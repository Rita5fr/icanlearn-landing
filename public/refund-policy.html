Refund & Return Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

At ICANLEARN, your satisfaction is our top priority. We stand behind the quality of our workshops and offer a clear, hassle-free refund policy.

🛡️

100% Money-Back Guarantee

If you don't find value in the workshop, we'll refund your full payment — no questions asked.

1. Workshop Registration Refund

Our workshops are priced at ₹49 per registration. We offer a 100% refund under the following conditions:

  • Before the workshop: If you are unable to attend, you may request a full refund any time before the workshop session begins.
  • During the workshop: If you attend the workshop and feel it did not provide value, notify us before the session ends and receive a full refund.

2. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, contact us through any of the following channels:

📧 Email: Help@icanlearn.in

📞 Phone: +91 9315447145

💬 WhatsApp: +91 9315447145

Please include the following in your refund request:

  • Your full name (as used during registration)
  • The email or phone number used for registration
  • Reason for refund (optional)

3. Refund Processing

  • Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days from the date of the request.
  • The refund will be credited back to the original payment method used during registration (UPI, card, net banking, etc.).
  • You will receive a confirmation email from Razorpay once the refund is initiated.

4. Non-Refundable Scenarios

Refunds will not be provided in the following cases:

  • Refund requested after the workshop has ended (more than 24 hours post-session)
  • The workshop was attended in full without any prior complaint raised during the session

5. Cancellation by ICANLEARN

If ICANLEARN cancels or reschedules a workshop for any reason:

  • All registered participants will be notified via WhatsApp and Email
  • You will have the option to transfer your registration to the next available batch or receive a full refund

6. Nature of Service

As this is a digital educational service (live online workshop), there are no physical products involved. Therefore, there are no shipping, exchange, or return policies applicable. This policy covers only the refund of registration fees.

7. Contact for Refund Queries

For any questions about our refund policy, please reach out:

  • Phone: +91 9315447145
